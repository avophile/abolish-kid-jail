Every Child Deserves Support

If you believe your child may need special education services, federal law gives you the right to request a free, comprehensive evaluation from your school district. This evaluation is the first step toward getting an Individualized Education Program (IEP) that provides the specialized support your child needs to succeed.

This tool helps you generate a professional evaluation request letter to ensure the school follows the law and evaluates your child promptly. Your written request starts the legal timeline - the school must respond within a reasonable time.
