Stop the School-to-Prison Pipeline

When your child with an IEP faces suspension or other disciplinary action for more than 10 days, federal law requires the school to hold a manifestation determination hearing. This hearing determines whether the behavior was caused by their disability or by the school's failure to implement their IEP.

This tool helps you generate a professional demand letter to ensure the school follows the law and protects your child from discriminatory discipline.
